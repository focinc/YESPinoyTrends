Top Things to Do in Barangay Baclaran

  • Visit Barangay Baclaran Church: This iconic Redemptorist Church is a place of prayer and pilgrimage, especially on Wednesdays for the Novena.
  • Explore the Street Markets: Barangay Baclaran is a shopping haven where you can find everything from clothes and accessories to home goods at unbeatable prices.
